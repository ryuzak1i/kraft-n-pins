⋆𑣲 Got Questions?

Here's everything you need to know!

Can I customize my own design for the button pins?
Absolutely! That's the whole point of this business — to bring your ideas to life! You can send us your own design, logo, photo, or concept, and we'll turn it into a button pin made just for you.
How do I send my design?
You can send your design to our Instagram:@kraftnpins or Email: kraftnpins@gmail.com. We accept direct files in PNG, JPEG, or even files stored in Google Drive. Make sure your image is clear and high-quality to get the best results!
What if I don't have a design yet? Can you make one for me?
Yes! We offer basic design services for free. Just tell us your concept or theme, and we'll help create it for you. For more complex designs, an additional fee may apply.
Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
No minimum for standard orders — you can order even just one single pin! For bulk/custom event orders (giveaways, school/org merch), message us for discounted bundle pricing!
How long does it take to process my order?
✦ Standard orders: 1-2 business days.
✦ Bulk orders: 5-10 business days (depends on quantity).
✦ Rush orders? Let us know and we'll try our absolute best to accommodate you!
How will I receive my pins?
✦ School/Nearby Meet-ups: Catch us around our regular spots in Imus, Cavite, or Manila for zero-cost handling!
✦ Same-Day Couriers: Lalamove or Grab for nearby areas if you need your pins ASAP.
❗ NOTE: Shipping or delivery fees are paid by the buyer and will vary depending on your location and chosen courier ❗

If your question isn't listed here, feel free to message us anytime — we're happy to help! 💌
