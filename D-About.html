about us

two sisters, one passion

Hi there, bestie! Welcome to Kraft N' Pins! We are two sisters who officially started this little passion project back in February 2025.


As students ourselves, we completely get the obsession with collecting anik-anik — because let's be real, our school IDs, tote bags, and backpacks look a million times better when they're decked out in cute stuff that shows off our personality!


We wanted to create an arts and crafts space that is 100% student-friendly. That means no scary minimum orders, no overwhelming prices, just pure creativity tailored to your budget.


Whether you're repping your favorite anime, showing off your fandom, flexing your school org, or just want a picture of your pet on your lanyard, we're here for it! Based happily in Imus, Cavite and Manila, we are always ready to bring your imagination to life.
